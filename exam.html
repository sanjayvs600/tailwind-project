Wellness Exam

Comprehensive health check-up for your pets.

What is a Wellness Exam?

A wellness exam is a routine examination performed by a veterinarian to assess your pet's overall health. It helps in early detection of any potential health issues and ensures that your pet is in the best possible condition.

What is Included?

  • Physical examination of your pet
  • Checking vital signs (heart rate, breathing, temperature)
  • Assessment of eyes, ears, nose, and throat
  • Dental evaluation
  • Weight check and nutritional advice
  • Vaccination updates if necessary
  • Parasite control recommendations

Why is it Important?

Regular wellness exams are crucial for maintaining your pet's health and well-being. They allow for early intervention and treatment of any potential health issues, which can lead to a longer and happier life for your furry friend.
