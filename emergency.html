Emergency Care

Urgent care for your pet's unexpected medical needs.

When to Seek Emergency Care

Your pet may require emergency care if they experience any of the following:

  • Trauma or Injury
  • Difficulty Breathing
  • Severe Bleeding
  • Loss of Consciousness
  • Seizures
  • Uncontrollable Vomiting or Diarrhea
  • Difficulty Urinating
  • Ingestion of Toxic Substances

What to Do in an Emergency

In case of an emergency, remain calm and contact our veterinary hospital immediately. Follow any instructions provided by our staff for transporting your pet safely.

Our Emergency Services

Our veterinary team is equipped to handle a wide range of emergency situations. We are committed to providing prompt and effective care for your pet during critical times.
