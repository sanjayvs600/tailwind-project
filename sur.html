Surgery

Safe and skilled surgical procedures for your pets.

Our Surgical Services

At Our Veterinary Hospital, we offer a range of surgical procedures to address various health concerns in pets. Our experienced surgical team is dedicated to providing the highest standard of care before, during, and after surgery.

  • Spaying and Neutering
  • Soft Tissue Surgery
  • Orthopedic Surgery
  • Dental Surgery
  • Tumor Removal
  • Emergency Surgery

Preparing Your Pet for Surgery

Before any surgical procedure, our team will provide detailed instructions on how to prepare your pet. This may include fasting before the surgery, ensuring they are up-to-date on vaccinations, and any specific pre-surgical care requirements.

Post-Surgery Care

After the surgery, we will provide you with detailed post-operative care instructions to ensure your pet recovers smoothly. Our team will be available to answer any questions or concerns you may have during the recovery period.

Schedule a Consultation

If your pet requires surgery, schedule a consultation with our veterinary team to discuss the best treatment plan for their specific needs.
